Coffee Challenge

The coffee drinking challenge is a timed competition where participants attempt to drink the most number of coffees within one minute. This challenge is popular among coffee enthusiasts and is often held in cafes, coffee shops, and events. The goal is to see who can drink the most coffee within the allotted time and is often used as a fun and playful way to test one's ability to handle large quantities of caffeine.
